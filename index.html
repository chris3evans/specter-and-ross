Specter & Ross

Solution designs offered through our seasoned lawyers, accountants and executives. Focus on growing your business while we take care of administration, finance and legal.

Specter & Ross is a solutions firm concerned with designing and implimenting solutions for S&M companies and investors. All enterprises we work with go through an integrated and multi-stage process led by a qualitative team of our specialists (legal, executive, and accounting).

Our areas of expertise are practices in the Zakat, Tax and Customs Authority, and the General Secretariat for Tax and Zakat Committees. Specter & Ross also has extensive experience in the field of establishing and managing investment companies - both domestic and foreign.

Specter & Ross has been the framework for solutions in established companies and investors across the world, including: Walmart, Coca Cola, Vangaurd and Netflix. We work with clients the entire way, from incorporation work and legal representation to team reviews and audits.
